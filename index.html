Workout Programs

Curated routines for every experience level.

Foundation Full Body

3 days/week. Focus on form and building base strength.

  • Goblet Squats (3x10)
  • Push-ups (3xMax)
  • Dumbbell Rows (3x10)

Core & Cardio Basics

2 days/week. Build stamina and a solid core.

  • Treadmill Walk/Jog (20 min)
  • Planks (3x30 sec)
  • Russian Twists (3x15)

Push/Pull/Legs Hypertrophy

6 days/week. Maximum volume for serious muscle gain.

  • Barbell Bench Press (4x8)
